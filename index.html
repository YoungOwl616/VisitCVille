Top 3 activities to do in Charlottesville

Visit Charlottesville's Historic Downtown Mall

The Downtown Mall is a vibrant collection of more than 120 shops and 30 restaurants housed in historic buildings along the city's Main Street.

Go for a picnic in a local park

Charlottesville has dozens of scenic, breathtaking parks that are just waiting for you to visit and enjoy.

Take a tour of the historic U.V.A. campus

The University of Virginia is a public research university in Charlottesville, Virginia. Founded in 1819 by Thomas Jefferson.

Your Guide: The Ghost of Thomas Jefferson

The ghost of Thomas Jefferson still resides in Charlottesville and offers free tours of the historic city. It is rumored that he will show travellers the location of his hidden treasure if they can succesfully answer 3 riddles. To date, no one has succesfully answered all 3 riddles.
